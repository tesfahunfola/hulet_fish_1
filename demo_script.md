# Etxplore App Demo Script (5-Minute Video)

**Total Duration:** 5 minutes  
**Style:** Fast-paced, engaging, focus on core sustainable tourism features  
**Note:** Assume user is already logged in. Avoid any sign-up/sign-in screens.

---

## [0:00 - 0:30] Introduction (30 seconds)

**Visual:** Fade in to app logo, then home page with Ethiopian landscapes  
**Voiceover/Narration:**  
"Welcome to Etxplore - your gateway to sustainable tourism in Ethiopia. Discover authentic experiences while making a positive environmental impact. Let's explore the core features that make Etxplore unique."

**On-screen text:** "Etxplore: Explore Ethiopia Sustainably"

---

## [0:30 - 1:30] Tour Discovery & Browsing (1 minute)

**Visual:** Navigate through home page, scroll through featured tours, filter options  
**Voiceover/Narration:**  
"Start your journey on our home page, showcasing Ethiopia's most incredible destinations. Browse curated tours featuring cultural experiences, natural wonders, and local cuisine. Use our filters to find tours by duration, difficulty, or price range."

**Actions to demonstrate:**  
- Scroll through featured tours  
- Click on filter buttons (show dropdowns)  
- Hover over tour cards to show quick preview  
- Click on a tour card to transition to details

**On-screen highlights:** Point to filter options, tour cards

---

## [1:30 - 2:30] Tour Details & Booking (1 minute)

**Visual:** Tour detail page - images, description, itinerary, pricing, book button  
**Voiceover/Narration:**  
"Dive deep into each tour with comprehensive details including day-by-day itineraries, included activities, and local expert guides. See the carbon footprint calculation for each tour, helping you make informed choices about your environmental impact."

**Actions to demonstrate:**  
- Scroll through tour images  
- Read itinerary sections  
- Show carbon emission estimate  
- Click "Book Now" button  
- Show booking form (focus on tour selection, dates, group size)  
- Complete booking process

**On-screen highlights:** Carbon footprint badge, booking confirmation

---

## [2:30 - 3:30] Carbon Offset System (1 minute)

**Visual:** Carbon offset page - calculator, offset options, payment  
**Voiceover/Narration:**  
"One of Etxplore's standout features is our integrated carbon offset system. Calculate your trip's emissions and choose from verified offset projects like reforestation or renewable energy initiatives. Every booking includes offset options to make your travel truly sustainable."

**Actions to demonstrate:**  
- Navigate to Carbon Offset page  
- Input trip details (distance, transport type)  
- Show emission calculation  
- Browse offset projects  
- Select and purchase offset  
- Show confirmation with impact metrics

**On-screen highlights:** Emission calculator, offset project cards, impact statistics

---

## [3:30 - 4:30] Eco-Score & Personal Impact (1 minute)

**Visual:** Eco-Score dashboard - personal metrics, achievements, progress charts  
**Voiceover/Narration:**  
"Track your environmental impact with our Eco-Score system. Earn points for sustainable choices like offsetting emissions, choosing eco-friendly tours, and participating in conservation activities. View your progress and compare with community averages."

**Actions to demonstrate:**  
- Navigate to Eco-Score page  
- Show personal score and breakdown  
- Scroll through achievements  
- View progress charts  
- Show leaderboard/community comparison

**On-screen highlights:** Score meter, achievement badges, progress graphs

---

## [4:30 - 5:00] Community Impact & Conclusion (30 seconds)

**Visual:** Community metrics page - collective statistics, global impact  
**Voiceover/Narration:**  
"See the collective impact of our community. Together, Etxplore users have offset thousands of tons of CO2 and supported numerous conservation projects. Join us in making tourism a force for good."

**Actions to demonstrate:**  
- Navigate to Community page  
- Show global statistics  
- Scroll through impact stories  
- End with call-to-action

**On-screen text:** "Together for Sustainable Tourism"  
**Final screen:** App logo with "Download Etxplore Today"

---

## Production Notes

- **Pacing:** Keep narration energetic and match visual transitions
- **Music:** Upbeat, inspiring background music with Ethiopian cultural elements
- **Transitions:** Smooth fades between sections
- **Text Overlays:** Use sparingly for key feature names
- **Demo Data:** Ensure all pages have realistic sample data
- **Technical Setup:** Record at 1080p, use screen recording software with voiceover

## Timing Breakdown
- Intro: 30s
- Tour Discovery: 60s
- Tour Details/Booking: 60s
- Carbon Offset: 60s
- Eco-Score: 60s
- Community/Conclusion: 30s
- **Total: 5 minutes**

This script focuses heavily on the core sustainable tourism functionalities while maintaining viewer engagement throughout the 5-minute duration.
